ZONG — Discord Anti-Raid & Server Security Bot

Partnership / Investment One-Pager


The Problem

Discord servers — especially gaming communities, NFT/crypto projects, and
growing brand servers — are frequent targets of "raids": compromised
admin accounts or malicious bots that mass-delete channels, mass-ban
members, or strip permissions in seconds, often destroying months of
community-building before a human moderator can react.

Most servers have no automated defense. By the time a mod notices, the
damage is done.

The Solution

ZONG is a Discord bot that detects raid patterns in real time — using
Discord's audit log to spot mass channel deletions, mass bans, and role
tampering — and automatically responds (kick / ban / quarantine) before
the raid can spread. It also includes:


Instant lockdown — freeze the whole server in one command
Backup & restore — snapshot channels/roles, rebuild in minutes if a raid gets through
Recent-joiner raid sweeps — catch and remove suspicious new accounts proactively
Configurable response modes — kick, ban, or quarantine, per server
